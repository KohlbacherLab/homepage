How To Find Us

By Car
  • Drive on B27 direction Tübingen from Stuttgart
  • Take the exit for Herrenberg, Rottenburg (A81)
  • Follow the street until you see an exit for "Zentrum" on the right
  • After crossing the bridge, take the next exit on the right
  • Continue on the road until you see a large building on the left labeled "SYSS" (see image)
By Train
  • Arrive at Tübingen train station
  • The main bus station is directly across from the station exit
  • Walk to the building (see image)
By Airplane
  • Arrive at Stuttgart airport
  • Take bus lines 826 or 828 (called 'Airport-Sprinter') from in front of the arrivals exit
  • Arrive at Tübingen main bus station
  • Walk to the building (see image)
